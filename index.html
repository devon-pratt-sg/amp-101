Why French Bulldogs Make Perfect Pets

French Bulldogs, affectionately known as "Frenchies," are one of the most beloved dog breeds in the world. Originally bred in England and refined in France, these charming companions have captured hearts with their distinctive bat ears, smooshed faces, and incredibly loving personalities.
